Resources

A curated list of the organizations, researchers, and advocates working on the front lines of cetacean consciousness, conservation, and communication.

Organizations

Whale and Dolphin Conservation (WDC)

Focus: Global protection, policy advocacy, and public education to end captivity and stop whaling.

Ocean Alliance

Focus: Whale research, bioacoustics, and ocean pollution, known for the SnotBot project.

Sea Shepherd Conservation Society

Focus: Direct action campaigns against illegal whaling and fishing operations worldwide.

The Dolphin Project

Focus: Ending dolphin captivity and slaughter, with a major focus on the hunts in Taiji, Japan.

Project CETI

Focus: Using cutting-edge AI and robotics to listen to and translate the communication of sperm whales.

NRDC - Marine Mammal Protection

Focus: Legal and scientific advocacy to reduce threats like ship strikes and ocean noise pollution.

International Marine Mammal Project

Focus: Known for the original "Dolphin-Safe" tuna campaign and continued anti-captivity efforts.

International Whaling Commission (IWC)

Focus: The global regulatory body for whaling that also addresses whale conservation and welfare.
