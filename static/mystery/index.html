The Two-Brain Mystery

How the Minds of Whales and Dolphins Challenge Our Dead Universe

For millions of years, a second, alien intelligence has been evolving in our oceans. The minds of whales and dolphins—vast, ancient, and organized around principles entirely different from our own—present a profound challenge to our most basic assumptions about reality. This work explores how their existence forces us to confront the possibility that consciousness is not a human accident, but a fundamental principle woven into the fabric of the cosmos itself.
