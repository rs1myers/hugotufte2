3.4Standard model still prevails

Text

One of the primary criticisms of Capra’s Tao of Physics, and indirectly of the new worldview, is that the Standard Model of particle physics is now almost universally accepted by the physics community, with alternative views such as the Bootstrap Model advocated by Capra pushed into obscurity. Oddly, none of the essays in “…Worldview…” addressed this issue. One possible explanation is that Capra and others expect that their conception will be borne out in mathematics, that the truth of the matter requires mathematical support or description. Why this must be so is not clear. It does not, however, seem unreasonable that a consistent and self-contained model of particle physics could exist in harmony with a broader, some would say mystical, perspective of mind and consciousness.
